Our Story

A Promise Made
in Gamagara, 2023

MAGABE CAPITAO ENTERPRISE (PTY) Ltd was born from a simple but powerful belief — that every client deserves to see their vision turned into a long-lasting reality. Founded in 2023 in the heart of the Gamagara District, we set out as a small, dedicated team with big ambitions and an unshakeable work ethic.

What began as a focused construction operation quickly grew into a multi-service enterprise. Today, we deliver outstanding construction, catering, and cleaning services — all under one roof, and all bound by the same principles that started us: values, respect, and clean execution.

Our experienced professionals bring not just skills to every project, but genuine care for the community they serve. We are your neighbours in Gamagara, and we build like it.

"To turn every client's vision into a long-lasting reality."

— Our founding promise, still kept every day
